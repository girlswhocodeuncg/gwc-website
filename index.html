About Girls Who Code

We are a College Loop chapter of Girls Who Code (GWC), a national nonprofit working to close the gender gap in technology. Our chapter is made up of women and non-binary students who are passionate about computer science and tech.

Together, we cultivate an encouraging space where members become career ready, support each other academically, and build lasting connections. Through bi-weekly meetings and hands-on events, we strengthen our skills, share opportunites, and celebrate our progress.
